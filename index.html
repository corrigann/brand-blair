A little bit about me.

Hi! My name is Blair Tyrrell, local to Oklahoma City and doing hair for 10 years. I am passionate about hair because the possibilities are endless and having the ability to create a new look for someone while tailoring it to their lifestyle is something I find very intriguing. A person has a physical connection to their hair, and often an emotional one as well so finding the right hairstyle to not only suit their features but enhance their self confidence brings joy to me personally. Helping someone achieve their hair goals makes me feel like I’m making the world a better place because I’m making each persons world better for them.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I’ve made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
